Who's this method for?

Home

If you find that you rely heavily on repetition, memorization, or set in stone ways of doing stuff, then just know that you will find it more difficult to achieve this method. This method is more for people who learn fast, learners that prefer understanding over memorization, people who get bored with long work hours for stuff not required. If you fit one or more of these categories, then you are more likely to find this method more suitable and easier to adopt. But even if you fit all the things I have outlined, you may fine that this method just doesn't click. So just know that it is not a guarantee that this is right for you.
